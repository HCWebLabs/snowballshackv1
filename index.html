What We Serve

Something Cold for Everyone

All the good stuff, made fresh at the window. Ask for our full flavor sheet when you pull up — there's way more than fits on a sign!

  • Snowballs & Shaved Ice

    Our signature. Heaps of finely shaved ice drenched in bold, bright syrups. Ask for condensed milk on top — an absolute game changer. Dozens of specialty named flavors including the legendary Rainbow.

    Fan Favorite

  • Hand-Dipped Milkshakes

    Thick, creamy, made to order. The Strawberry Cheesecake shake has a full-on cult following in Anderson County. Worth every minute of the wait — and there might be one.

    Must Try

  • Soft Serve Ice Cream

    Classic cones, cups, and twists made with quality local ice cream. Simple, cold, and perfectly satisfying after a long hot Tennessee afternoon. Kids absolutely love it.

    Classic

  • Slushies

    Ice-cold and impossibly refreshing. The perfect cool-down after the pool, the lake, or surviving a brutal East Tennessee summer afternoon. Multiple flavors, zero regrets.

    Ice Cold

  • Smoothies

    Blended fresh and fruity. Cold, delicious, and slightly more virtuous than a milkshake. You're still getting a snowball too — don't even pretend otherwise.

    Fresh & Fruity

  • Banana Splits & Ice Cream Nachos

    Loaded banana splits your way — hold what you don't like, double what you love. Plus our famous ice cream nachos. Yes, they're a real thing. No, you won't regret it.

    Only at Sweet P's

The Sweet P's Experience

Clinton's Official Summer Tradition

After the game. After the pool. After a long week. When East Tennessee heat peaks, everyone ends up at the blue shack on Seivers Blvd.

  • Post-Game Ritual

    Little League just ended. Both teams know where the next stop is. Sweet P's — obviously.

  • After the Pool

    Salty, sunburned, and absolutely ready for a snowball. Shaded picnic tables and big umbrellas — perfect cool-down spot.

  • Golden Hour

    The Open sign glowing, the lot filling up, kids running in the grass. Pure East Tennessee summer magic.
